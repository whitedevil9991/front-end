Image in Html
 
<img> tag

Atrribute:Align,Alt,Border,Height,src,width,hspace,vspace

Align:aligns it to the center or left or right

Border:sets the border

hspace:sets the horizontal space

vspace:sets the vertical space

alt:alt="image not found" the alt is for when there is error in loading of the picture.

src:Defines name and path of the image file.

    eg.<img src="flower.jpg" alt="this is image"/>
       <img src="c:\vaishali\flower.jpg" alt="this is image"/>

<img src="flower.jpg" alt="this is image" width="50" 
       height="50" Border="2" align="right">



use image as links

  <a href="http://www.facebook.com"><img src="\picture\Images\fimg.jpg" height="50" width="50"/></a>
